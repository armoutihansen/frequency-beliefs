## Correctness concerns

- The quantile-index logic in `quantile_index_range` correctly uses `j*(p)=min{j:S_j(p)≥τ}` with the equivalences `min_p j* = min{j:sup S_j ≥ τ}` and `max_p j* = max{j: inf S_{j-1} < τ}` (with `S_0=0`). The brute-force-containment check is the right direction (reverse direction is grid-tolerance-bound, which the docstring states).
- For exact/squared rules, the cum-sum bounds use exact LP over the closed-form polytope. For Manhattan, the bounds come from threshold-slice union with a greedy box-LP per slice; the tightness depends on `c_denom` (2000 in core check, 4000 in §3.6 reproduction). Documented as such.
- The L1 cum-sum bound is union of slice bounds; this is correct because the identified set decomposes as the union over `c∈[0,1]` of CDF-defined boxes (per the manuscript's threshold characterization).

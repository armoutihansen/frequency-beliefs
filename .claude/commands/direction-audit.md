Audit a proposed project direction before it is adopted.

Steps:

1. Read:
   - `_context/current_status.md`
   - `_context/current_issues.md`
   - `_context/exploration/direction_memos.md`
   - `_context/exploration/literature_scan_log.md`
   - relevant paper sections

2. Evaluate the proposed direction using:
   - `research-strategist`
   - `literature-reviewer`
   - `theory-auditor` if formal results are involved
   - `simulation-methodologist` if simulations are involved

3. Classify the direction as:
   - adopt now;
   - adopt after proof check;
   - adopt after literature check;
   - promising but too broad;
   - promising but not this paper;
   - appendix/extension only;
   - reject.

4. Report:
   - decision;
   - rationale;
   - required changes;
   - required checks;
   - risks;
   - next concrete step.

5. If rejected, append a concise entry to:
   `_context/exploration/rejected_directions.md`

Do not edit the paper unless explicitly asked.
